Design Plan

I plan to use the primary colors scheme which consist of blue and white to create a clean, classy, and modern look. I will also chose white because it emphasizes the business professionalism, which is a necessity for this chamber of commerce website. I will design this site with sans-serif fonts, such as Arial, Monserrat nsans Serif, or Roboto, to create a simple and nice look for my website. I will make use of contrasting font sizes to create visual hierarchy, so that people can easily notice the pages to visit. These would be headlines and navigation tabs they must have bigger fonts from the rest of the text to grab visitors' attention and guide them through the site. When designed this way, the website will look modern, clean, and easy to read while sending the right message about the organization's purpose.
