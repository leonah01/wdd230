About Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe , officially Republic of Zimbabwe formerly Rhodesia, Landlocked country, southern Africa. The Zambezi River forms the country northwestern boundary and contains Victoria Falls as well as the Kariba Dam. Agricultural products, livestock, and mineral reserves, including gold, are all economically important. We have many torist visiting the area to see the mega Victoria Falls and Game as well as the naturally built homes, attracting millions of visitors each year.
